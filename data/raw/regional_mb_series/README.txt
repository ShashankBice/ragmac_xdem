Mass balance time series estimated from in-situ and geodetic measurements, and for RGI regions, as in Zemp et al., 2019 (DOI:10.1038/s41586-019-1071-0).
Data updated to the period 2016-2020, provided by M. Zemp in January 2022.
See also https://wgms.ch/sea-level-rise/ for some details.

- regional_adhoc_estimates_BAs_anom_corr_mwe.csv contains the MB series for each region, in mwe
- regional_adhoc_estimates_BAs_anom_corr_error_mwe.csv contains the associated error
- regional_adhoc_estimates_BA_anom_mwe.csv contains the MB anomalies w.r.t the 2006-2016 time period
